Mohamed Jendoubi, also known as Psyco M, was born on July 10, 1986,in Ibn Sina, Tunis. He is a rapper whose music genre is hip-hop. Inspired by Lotfi Double Canon's songs, he embarked on his career in 1999. Psyco M gained his first hits in 2011 with the release of his song "Manipulation." then, in 2017, he achieved further success with "Plume." His latest albums, namely "Honneur," "Solide," and "Fusion," were met with tremendous acclaim and achieved high levels of success. Throughout his career. He enjoyed great popularity, particularly in the inland regions.
